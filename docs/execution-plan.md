# Agri-Tech Farm: Phased Execution & Financial Plan

This document outlines the milestone-based execution strategy for the 1-acre autonomous farm, detailing the estimated duration, action items, and funding breakdown (Bank Loan vs. Founder's Margin Money) for each phase.

## Phase 0: The Foundation & Land Acquisition

**Estimated Duration:** 6 to 8 weeks (Active time)

*Note: An extended break can be taken after this phase is complete to finalize the strategy before triggering the loan and starting Phase 1.*

- Register the 4-partner Limited Liability Partnership (LLP).
- Acquire the 1-acre agricultural land.
- Hire an agricultural consultant to draft the Detailed Project Report (DPR) needed for the bank loan and subsidy applications.

| Funding Category | Estimated Amount (INR) |
|---|---|
| Total Estimated Cost | ₹15,50,000 - ₹20,50,000 |
| **Founder's Money (100% Out of pocket)** | **₹15.5 Lakhs - ₹20.5 Lakhs** |
| Bank Loan | ₹0 (Applied for, but not disbursed until Phase 1) |

## Phase 1: Base Civil & Utilities

**Estimated Duration:** 8 to 10 weeks

*This phase begins when you are ready to break ground and the bank releases the first loan tranche.*

- Dig the borewell and install the massive 50,000L water storage.
- Excavate the pit, build the concrete retaining walls, and lower the two 40-foot shipping containers for the bunkers.
- Set up the central electrical grid, standalone solar canopy, and internet connection.

| Funding Category | Estimated Amount (INR) |
|---|---|
| Total Estimated Cost | ₹20,00,000 - ₹30,00,000 |
| **Founder's Money (20% Margin)** | **₹4.0 Lakhs - ₹6.0 Lakhs** |
| Bank Loan (80% Disbursement) | ₹16.0 Lakhs - ₹24.0 Lakhs |

## Phase 2: The Core Ecosystem

**Estimated Duration:** 8 to 12 weeks

*This is the most capital-intensive phase where the revenue-generating engines are built.*

- Erect the 30,000 sq. ft. surface greenhouse.
- Install the Recirculating Aquaculture System (RAS) fish tanks, biofilters, and vertical hydroponic grow beds.
- Build the 3,000 sq. ft. dairy shed, install the biogas digester, and bring in the 3 to 5 cows.

| Funding Category | Estimated Amount (INR) |
|---|---|
| Total Estimated Cost | ₹45,00,000 - ₹65,00,000 |
| **Founder's Money (20% Margin)** | **₹9.0 Lakhs - ₹13.0 Lakhs** |
| Bank Loan (80% Disbursement) | ₹36.0 Lakhs - ₹52.0 Lakhs |

## Phase 3: The Tech Stack & Operations

**Estimated Duration:** 4 to 6 weeks

*The final stretch where you integrate the automation and plant the first seeds. Once complete, government inspections take place to trigger your massive subsidies.*

- The Tech Department installs the ESP32s, sensors (NPK, pH, PT100), and automated dosing pumps across all nodes.
- Procure the first batch of fish fingerlings, saffron corms, mushroom spores, and exotic seeds.
- Boot up the Node-RED servers and InfluxDB to start the autonomous cycles.

| Funding Category | Estimated Amount (INR) |
|---|---|
| Total Estimated Cost | ₹5,00,000 - ₹10,00,000 |
| **Founder's Money (20% Margin + Initial OpEx)** | **₹1.0 Lakh - ₹2.0 Lakhs** |
| Bank Loan (80% Disbursement) | ₹4.0 Lakhs - ₹8.0 Lakhs |
